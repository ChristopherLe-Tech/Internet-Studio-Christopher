Where to Eat in Night City

Afterlife Bar

The legendary hangout of fixers, mercs, and netrunners. Grab a ‘Johnny Silverhand’ cocktail and soak in the neon atmosphere. You’ll pay extra for the atmosphere and the bragging rights.

Tom's Diner

A local diner spot. Grab a cup of synth-coffee and a greasy breakfast while listening to mercs whisper about their latest gigs. The food’s average but the atmosphere’s authentic.

El Coyote Cojo

Family-run cantina famous for tequila, street tacos, and fistfights that usually start by midnight. Great drinks, great music, and even better people, if you’re on their good side.
