About Night City

Night City, located in the Free State of Northern California, is the most vibrant metropolis of the 2070s. A place where neon lights never fade, and ambition burns brighter than the sun. In this cybernetic haven, technology and humanity blend into one unforgettable urban experience.

Whether you’re here for chrome, culture, or chaos, Night City has something for everyone: sprawling mega-buildings, world-class entertainment, and a skyline that never sleeps.

Highlights

  • Explore neon-lit streets and futuristic architecture
  • Experience the latest in braindance entertainment
  • Tour iconic districts like Watson and Japantown
  • Ride through the city in your own cybercar
  • Experience the chaotic nature of its citizens from streetkids, nomads, and corpos
