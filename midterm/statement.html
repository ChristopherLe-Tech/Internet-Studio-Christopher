Design Statement

My design draws inspiration from the cyberpunk aesthetic as the high-tech, high-contrast visuals with vibrant neon blues and yellows. The typeface “Orbitron” and "Audiowide" from the Google Web Fonts Helper captures the futuristic feel of Night City, while “Open Sans” keeps text readable.

I used dark backgrounds and bold button colors to evoke the atmosphere of the game’s world, where light is scarce and technology defines beauty. With that contrast of colors, I've added a button animation of where they glow as you hover them to mimic the neon lights of Night City.The layout is structured and simple, mirroring the rigid and defined architecture of Night City.

The images are official pictures from the game as they give the webpage a realistic standard to what is shown in Night City. The color palette reflects the city’s neon glow, mixing cool blue tones with vivid yellows for contrast.
