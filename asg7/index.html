About Anakin Skywalker

Anakin Skywalker, once a heroic Jedi Knight and skilled pilot, rose from humble beginnings on Tatooine to becomeone of the most powerful figures in the galaxy.Known for his unmatched skill with the Force and a fierce determination to protect those he loves, Anakin is a legend who shaped the fate of the Republic.

Anakin would make a strong ruler because he is driven by passion, vision, and a deep desire to bring order and stability to a chaotic galaxy. His leadership would inspire unity and progress — ensuring peace through strength.

Learn more about his legacy on Anakin Skywalker’s Wikipedia page

Running Mate: Obi-Wan Kenobi

Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin’s former mentor, is wise, balanced, and trusted by both the Jedi and the people.His calm leadership and moral integrity perfectly complement Anakin’s intensity. Together, they would lead the galaxy toward a new era of unity and purpose.

Campaign Platform: Peace through Power

Anakin’s campaign centers around restoring peace and stability across the galaxy.His platform focuses on ending corruption within the Senate, strengthening planetary defenses, and ensuring safety for all systems. He envisions a galaxy where justice and order reign supreme as a new golden age for every citizen of the Republic.
