My Favorite Movies

These twelve movies have stuck with me because of their storytelling, visuals, characters, and emotional impact. Each one has influenced the way I see the world, and this gallery showcases what I love about them most.

Real Steel

A futuristic action drama where a washed-up boxer and his son rebuild their relationship while training a scrappy robot fighter. It’s my favorite underdog stories of all time.

Kung Fu Panda 2

Po faces a powerful new villain while uncovering the truth about his past. The film is a perfect sequel as it all revolves around him finding his inner peace and how to overcome it.

Interstellar

A team of astronauts travels through a wormhole to save humanity as Earth collapses. I would consider it the magum opus of realistic science fiction.

21 Jumpstreet

A comedy buddy cop movie where two cops go undercover as highschool students to stop a drug ring. It's incredibly funny and stupid, just my kind of humor.

Weathering With You

A runaway boy meets a girl who can control the weather, and together they face the consequences of her power. It's a visually beautiful story of romance with an amazing soundtrack that I still listen to.

Guardians of the Galaxy

A group of misfit criminals becomes an unlikely team as they try to stop a cosmic threat. It’s a fun, colorful space adventure full of humor, found family, and great music.

Rogue One: A Star Wars Story

A band of rebels risks everything to steal the Death Star plans. Gritty, emotional, and action-packed, I believed its one of the most grounded and powerful stories in the Star Wars universe. It almost makes me forget its a space oprea Star Wars movie. (PS. Watch Andor too)

Mulan

A young woman disguises herself as a soldier to protect her family and save China. The film celebrates courage, identity, and honor with memorable characters and music. One of my first ever disney movies that I really connected with.

Your Name

An anime about two strangers mysteriously begin swapping bodies and form a deep connection across time. Another visually stunning movie about romance, fate, and longing with another you never met before.

Pokémon: The First Movie

One of the very first Pokemon movies that tells a story of Ash, Pikachu and friends face the first ever cloned pokemon Mewtwo. The film is what started my love for Pokemon and still to this day.

The Lego Batman Movie

A comedic lego movie about the caped crusader, which shows a different side of the batman persona we all grown to know. I believe its one of the best depictions of Batman in recent years.

Kung Fu Hustle

A parody chaotic martial-arts comedy where a wannabe gangster gets swept into a battle between masters. It's over the top action is what made me get into martial arts films of the like.
